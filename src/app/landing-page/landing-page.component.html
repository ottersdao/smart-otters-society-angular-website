About Us

We are a Society of SmArt Otters in love with innovative artworks. We want to aid young and talented artists to have a platform to present their work to the world. We check out artworks, choose the finest ones and bring them forward to the world.
To achieve this goal, we rolled up our sleeves and started our mission to create a Decentralized Autonomous Art Gallery.
Become a member of the SmArt Otters Society so we can redefine the role of dealers as the bridge between artists and collectors. Join our decentralized art gallery as a SmArt Otter and take part in this historical turn. We hope that our unique gallery creates a bright future for the art world and young artists.

Our Works

SmArt Otters Society NFTs are 10,000 machine-generated hand-drawn and unique Otters created by a young and talented artist named Moineau. The Otters are made from more than 300 objects in 8 layers. They swim across Ethereum blockchain and plan to run a decentralized autonomous art gallery together.
In the SmArt Otters Society, four separate Otters groups live in harmony. Despite their unique features, they have some in common. They are all passionate about art in such a way that their eyes sparkle at the sight of a remarkable piece.

Rarity

Critics might appear straight face on the outside, but they possess a genuinely aesthetic soul. They try to combine their instinct and knowledge to discover a highly complex richness of art.

Punks are rebellious in nature and want to experience life on their own terms. They enjoy avant-garde nonconformist artworks.

Hippies are spiritual, tired of busy city life, and love long walks in nature. They love to explore the mystery of the art world

Fantasies are cheerful. They search for uplifting experiences in the art. They love pieces dealing with everyday objects and something worth hanging in a museum.

Life Plan

We see the SmArt Otters Society as a living creature. This creature has a mind and a soul as well as a physical body. That's why its life plan has two parts.
The Society as its mind and soul, and the System as its flesh.
Watching this creature mature and evolve with your own eyes is one of the exciting parts of engaging in the SmArt Otters Society.

FAQ

What is a DAO?

+ −

A DAO is an organization with a decentralized governance structure led by its members. The cornerstone of a DAO is its customized SmArt contract, a self-executing real-time computer program.

What is Otters DAO, and how can I join?

+ −

Otters DAO or the SmArt Otters DAO is an art gallery with a decentralized governance structure. To be a member of the SmArt Otters' Art Gallery and help build its future, holding a SmArt Otters' NFT is the first step. Each NFT represents one vote in the Otters DAO system.

What is the utility behind the SmArt Otters Society?

+ −

SmArt Otters Society members run the Otters DAO together and, with the help of a unique customized SmArt contract, make critical decisions about the future of their community. They make proposals about future operations and projects, vote on them, and select those that achieve some predefined consensus level.
